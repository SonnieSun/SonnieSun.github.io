Epic Quest: Chronicles

Timeline

Jan 2023 - Jun 2023

Role

Lead Game Designer & Developer

Project Overview

Epic Quest: Chronicles is an open-world action adventure game set in a fantasy realm filled with magic, mystery, and danger. The project aimed to create an immersive experience with engaging storytelling, dynamic combat, and breathtaking environments.

As lead designer, I was responsible for developing the core gameplay mechanics, quest design, character progression system, and overseeing the implementation of the open-world environment. The project involved a small team of 5 developers working over 6 months.

The game features a unique combat system that combines melee attacks, magic spells, and strategic dodging. Players can explore a vast open world with diverse biomes, each with its own challenges, creatures, and hidden treasures.

Project Details

  • Genre Action Adventure, Open World
  • Platforms PC, PlayStation, Xbox
  • Engine Unreal Engine 5
  • Team Size 5 Developers
  • Release Q4 2023

Technologies

Unreal Engine 5 C++ Blueprints Blender Substance Painter FMOD

Development Process

Concept & Design

We started with extensive world-building and concept development, creating the lore, character backstories, and initial level designs. This phase included creating a prototype to test core mechanics.

Jan - Feb 2023
Mar - Apr 2023

Core Development

Focused on implementing the core gameplay systems including combat mechanics, character progression, and quest systems. We also developed the open-world framework and basic environmental assets.

Content Creation

Expanded the world with additional environments, characters, and quests. Created and implemented art assets, animations, and sound design. Began playtesting with a small group of testers.

May 2023
Jun 2023

Polish & Release

Focused on bug fixing, performance optimization, and refining gameplay based on feedback. Prepared marketing materials and launched the game on multiple platforms.

Key Features

Vast Open World

Explore a massive open world with diverse biomes including forests, mountains, deserts, and ancient ruins, each with unique challenges and secrets.

Dynamic Combat

Fluid combat system combining melee attacks, ranged abilities, and powerful magic spells with strategic dodging and parrying mechanics.

Engaging Story

Immersive narrative with branching quests, memorable characters, and impactful choices that shape the world around you.

Character Customization

Deep character progression system with multiple classes, skill trees, and equipment customization options.

Puzzle Challenges

Environmental puzzles and riddles that test your problem-solving skills and reward exploration.

Original Soundtrack

Immersive musical score that adapts to gameplay situations and enhances the emotional impact of the story.
