## Project Overview

Blabber Town was ordinary—until a buried radio began receiving alien signals, turning everyone’s speech into nonsense. As the only unaffected outsider, you wield the “Nonsense Converter” gifted by the town’s cats in dream. It has the power to transform absurd chatter into purifying energy.
